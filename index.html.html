About Us

A Dental Practice Built on
Hygiene, Trust & Clinical Precision

32 Carat Dental Hospital is a multi-specialty dental care center located at Rajkamal Square, Amravati. Established with a mission to provide reliable, hygienic, and clinically advanced dental treatment, we have earned the trust of over 1,200 patients through consistent care and transparent practice.

Our clinic adheres to strict sterilization protocols, operates with modern diagnostic and treatment equipment, and maintains a clean, organized infrastructure that patients consistently acknowledge in their reviews.

  • Clean, hygienic clinical environment
  • Advanced sterilization protocols
  • Multi-specialty treatment capabilities
  • Supportive, patient-first staff
Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Treatments

From preventive care to advanced surgical procedures — every treatment delivered with precision, hygiene, and patient comfort.

General & Preventive Dentistry

Foundational dental care focused on early diagnosis, prevention, and maintaining long-term oral health.

  • Bleeding Gums Treatment

    Targeted therapy for gum inflammation and periodontal conditions to prevent further tissue damage.

  • Tooth Reshaping

    Conservative enamel recontouring to improve tooth alignment, shape and overall smile aesthetics.

  • Routine Dental Procedures

    Cleaning, scaling, polishing, cavity detection, and preventive check-ups for optimal oral hygiene.

Restorative Dentistry

Repairing damaged or decayed teeth to restore function, strength, and natural appearance.

  • Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

    Precise endodontic therapy to treat infected tooth pulp, relieve pain, and save the natural tooth structure.

  • Fixed Prosthodontics

    Custom-fitted crowns and bridges to restore damaged or missing teeth with durable, natural-looking results.

Advanced & Surgical Procedures

Complex oral surgical interventions performed with clinical precision and strict aseptic protocols.

  • Wisdom Tooth Extraction

    Safe surgical removal of impacted or problematic third molars with minimal discomfort and recovery time.

  • Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery

    Specialized surgical care for jaw disorders, facial injuries, and complex oral pathologies.

  • Maxillofacial Prosthetics

    Custom prosthetic rehabilitation for patients with facial or oral tissue defects, restoring form and function.

Implant & Modern Dentistry

Cutting-edge dental solutions using the latest technology for lasting, high-quality outcomes.

  • Dental Implant Fixing

    Permanent titanium-based tooth replacement that integrates with the jawbone for a natural, long-lasting result.

  • Laser Dentistry

    Minimally invasive, high-precision laser treatments for soft tissue procedures, reducing bleeding and healing time.
